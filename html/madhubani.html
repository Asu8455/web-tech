MADHUBANI

Madhubani in Bihar is famous for its art and culture. This city is known for Madhubani paintings which are spread all over the world. There are many temples to see here. Places to visit in Madhubani are Kapileshwar than, Sarath, Nagar fort, Bhawanipur, Uchaitha. The place experiences a very hot climate so it is better to visit here after the monsoon or during winters. The top places which are located near Madhubani are Gangtok, Darjeeling, Nalanda, Patna, and Vaishali. If you are a painting lover then this place is best for you. Here you see many different types of Madhubani paintings.
