NAVLAKHA PALACE

Navlakha palace is situated in Rajnaga near Madhubani. The place was built by Maharaja Maheshwar Singh. In 1934, the place was suffered from extensive destruction due to an earthquake. You can enjoy the archeological site, temples, and ponds here. The place is located on the Eastern bank of River Kamla. Temple of Goodness kali is located on the northern side of the pond.
